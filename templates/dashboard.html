KYC Verification Process


The purpose of KYC (Know Your Customer) Verification is establishing the identity of any customer. In online KYC the identity of the person is verified electronically via Aadhar-based (or any other ID) substantiation. Thus KYC provides proof of identity of the person to the service provider instantly, dispensing the need for the tedious in-person verification.




Complete your E-KYC Verfication in 3 Simple Steps :


STEP 1 : Login & click the "Start Process" button on your Dashboard

STEP 2 : Upload your aadhar card, driving license or any other ID

STEP 3 : Click "Open Camera" displayed on the screen and look into the camera till the process the completed

Your Verification process is completed and the result will be displayed on the screen
